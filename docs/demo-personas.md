## Buyer Persona (Audience)

**Typical titles:** VP Security, SOC Director, MDR Practice Lead, CISO (mid-market)

**What they care about:**
- Analyst efficiency without losing accountability
- SLA proof for contracts
- Client retention through visible value
- AI governance story for auditors and boards

**What they don't want:** Another SIEM, another SOAR, or black-box AI autopilot.
